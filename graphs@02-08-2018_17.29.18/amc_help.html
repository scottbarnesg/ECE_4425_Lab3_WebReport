Data Exchange Charts

The Data Exchange Chart displays the overall flow of application-layer data between tiers, from the start of the recorded data exchange (far left) to the end (far right).

A chart colors each application-message arrow and application-message-group bar by the amount of application-layer data stored in the corresponding message(s), as indicated by the "Application Payload Size" legend beneath the chart.
